My Psi Chi Involvement

In 2021, I was accepteed into Psi Chi- The International Honor Society in Psychology. To be accepted, applicants must be in the top 35% of their class and hold a GPA above 3.0. After being a member for a year, I was officially initiated into Psi Chi during the annual Initiation Ceremony that was origianlly postponed due to Covid. As a second semester Junior, I applied to become the President of Psi Chi and was elected by Psi Chi members into presidency for the 2022-2023 school year! I have been serving as the President ever since.

As the President of Psi Chi, I plan Psych Talks with professors and researchers, coordinate community service events, donation drives, and fundraising events, as well as holding general and Exec Board meetings. I also oversee membership applications and dues. I have thoroughly enjoyed my time so far as Psi Chi President and am proud to be Psi Chi member for life!

My Instagram Takeover Post From the UW Psychology Department's Account
